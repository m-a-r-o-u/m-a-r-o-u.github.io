## Research Cluster Onboarding Kit

Direct link: [/#projects/research-cluster-onboarding-kit](#projects/research-cluster-onboarding-kit)

A set of onboarding materials that reduce time-to-first-success on shared HPC systems.

- First-week checklist for accounts, storage, and scheduler basics.
- Starter templates for common CPU/GPU jobs.
- Troubleshooting guide for frequent setup and runtime issues.
